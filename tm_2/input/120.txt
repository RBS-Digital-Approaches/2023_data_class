August 2 , 1952 OBITUARY Ex-Slave 's Son , 78 , Financier , Is Dead Special to THE NEW YORK TIMES DURHAM , N. C. , Aug. 1 -- Charles Clinton Spaulding , president of five company comprise the large all-Negro enterprise in the world , die at his home here today on his seventy-eighth birthday. Mr. Spaulding , one of fourteen child of a former North Carolina slave , be a founder of the five concern -- three insurance company , a bank and a building and loan concern. The organization have admit asset of more than $ 33,000,000. At his death , he be a trustee of Howard University , Washington ; Shaw University , Raleigh , N. C. , and North Carolina College for Negroes , Durham. He receive four honorary degree from a many university , although he never attend college. Mr. Spaulding , together with a Negro barber and a Negro doctor , found the North Carolina Mutual Life Insurance Company in 1898. The barber , John Merrick , have Washington Duke , the tobacco executive , a a regular customer. Mr. Spaulding credit the advice the executive give from the barber chair with help the insurance company survive. Today the company have more than $ 150,000,000 in insurance in force. Surviving him be his widow , three son and a daughter. One of Richest U. S. Negroes DURHAM , N. C. , Aug. 1 ( AP ) -- Mr. Spaulding , who die here today , be recognize widely a one of the wealthy member of his race in this country. He be bear near Whiteville in rural Columbus County eleven year after President Lincoln have emancipate the slaves. His first job be a a $ 10-a-month dishwasher. Mr. Spaulding once recall that the first office for the North Carolina Mutual Life Insurance Company rent for two dollar a month. `` When I come into the office early in the morning , '' he say , `` I roll up my sleeve and sweep the place a janitor. Then I roll down my sleeve and be an agent , and later I put on my coat and become general manager . ''
