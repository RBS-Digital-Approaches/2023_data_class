January 28 , 1960 OBITUARY Beno Gutenberg , Seismologist , 70 Special to THE NEW YORK TIMES LOS ANGELES , Jan. 27 -- Dr. Beno Gutenberg , an authority on geophysics and seismology , die Monday night at Huntington Memorial Hospital. His age be 70. He be stricken with influenza over the week-end and the ailment develop into pneumonia. From 1947 until his retirement two year ago , Dr. Gutenberg be director of the Seismological Laboratory at the California Institute of Technology. He have be at Caltech since 1930. He be best know for his contribution to knowledge about the interior of the earth. At 23 , while a graduate student at the University of Goettingen in Germany , he make what be believe to be the first correct determination of the composition and size of the earth 's central core. He also contribute to the understanding of seismic wave , the magnitude of earthquake and the temperature of the stratosphere. Dr. Gutenberg be bear in Darmstadt , Germany. He receive his Ph.D. at Goettingen in 1911 and hold a series of academic post in Strasbourg and Frankfurt before come to Caltech in 1930. He be past president of the Seismological Society of America and a member of the National Academy of Sciences and the American Academy of Arts and Sciences .
