
The #1 New York Times Bestselling SeriesPerfect gift for fans of the Maze Runner series, this includes all three books in Brandon Sanderson’s Reckoners series—Steelheart, Firefight, and Calamity. Find out what James Dashner called “fantastic,” with “relentless” suspense and an “explosive” climax.   How far would you go for revenge if someone killed your father? If someone destroyed your city? If everything you ever loved was taken from you? David Charleston will go to any lengths to stop the Epics. But to exact revenge in this new world, David will need the Reckoners—a shadowy group of rebels bent on maintaining justice.
