
This volume collects issues #5–8 of Tales of the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles Volume 2, including the stories “Blind Faith,” “Scars,” “Darkness Weaves,” and “Virus” as well as back-up stories and pinups, all by classic Turtles writers and artists Jim Lawson, Dean Clarrain, Chris Allan, Eric Talbot, Steve Murphy, Dan Berger, and Peter Laird.
