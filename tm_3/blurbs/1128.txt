
Longarm’s got news for some slimy prospectors—and it ain’t good…She’s a loyal daughter, a hard worker, a wild lover—and for some vicious men, Cindy Allison is good as dead. In Denver, she’s been pinching pennies to save her parents’ Arizona ranch. With her six feet under, though, evil prospectors could buy it for a song…   But not if Deputy Marshal Custis Long can help it. After giving her bastard of a boss some lessons in how to treat a lady, Longarm will do everything he can to take down Cindy’s hit-man, with or without the handsome reward…
