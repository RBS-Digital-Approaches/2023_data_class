
Twins are a double blessing.   Brindle Cow is having a baby! All the delightful characters from Eve Bunting’s The Wedding are back to celebrate the happy event, along with some adorable new  characters. Don’t miss the exuberant—and numerous—baby rabbits. Judy Love’s illustrations are packed with humorous details as the  group of devoted friends show off their gifts and travel to Brindle  Cow’s baby shower. But when the friends arrive to greet the new  baby—surprise! surprise!—it’s twins.
