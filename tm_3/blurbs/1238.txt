
You’re on an awesome adventure through time! Where do you go? What do you do? It’s up to YOU! Filled with excitement and hilarity, this compositional challenge lets you tell your very own story starring…YOU! Combining National Geographic Kids’ photography and illustrations in colorful laugh-out-loud pages, this engaging, entertaining, and educational book introduces you to people and places from all throughout time, and invites you to enter a new world imaginatively by combining your story with theirs.
