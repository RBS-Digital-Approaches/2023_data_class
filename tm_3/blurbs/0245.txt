
Gon is a small dinosaur that has managed to survive extinction. He goes on fun adventures in a world of vibrant creatures and fauna.PREDATORS BEWARE! THIS LITTLE GUY IS OUT TO DEFEND THE DEFENSELESS!65 million years ago, dinosaurs experienced aw catastrophic extinction that ended their dominance on Earth except for one! Gon’s quest to conquer the wilderness continues. In this volume, he takes to the ocean and rides a shark – from the inside! Back on land he is tormented by a tick and shakes up a forest. And finally, Gon marches with the penguins! Told entirely without words, these stories highlight the amazingly detailed art and visual storytelling genius of creator Masashi Tanaka.Includes special extras after the story!
