
Three classic Little Golden Book nursery tales are now available together in one deluxe volume!  Published in celebration of Little Golden Books’ 75th anniversary, this collection gathers together three of the most beloved Little Golden Books of all time: The Three Bears, The Little Red Hen, and Puss in Boots. Illustrated by two Little Golden Book greats—J. P. Miller and Feodor Rojankovsky—this beautiful book of nursery tales, with its glossy pages and gold-foil spine, is a must-have for every child’s bookshelf.
